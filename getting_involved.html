Getting Involved With The Team

The best way to get started is to join our discord server! This will keep you up to date with the latest team meetings, events, and contests.


Joining Our Discord Server

You can join our discord server by clicking the link here. This link is limited to 100 invites and may expire - if it doesn't work, please email idehaan@umich.edu for an updated link.


Team Activities

  • Weekly meetings to discuss algorithms and their implementations for various programming challenges. Show up and discuss problem statements, descriptions, methods of attack, as well as sample solutions!
  • Competitions and occasional practices, often with other people from universities located across North America.
  • Communication regarding meeting time and location is sent through the discord, so make sure to join!

Who is Welcome?

Anyone! Undergraduate, graduate, and even high school students are welcome to join. We are a group that loves to solve programming challenges!


Do I Need to Compete?

No. You do not need to compete in order to be involved with the team! Although preparing for programming contests is an important aspect of the team, you are encouraged to join our discussions even if you do not want to compete.
